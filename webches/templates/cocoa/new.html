What is?

WEBCHES is a cocoa based clinic that gives information on cocoa diseases and likely solutions for each given disease.

Farmers

WEBCHES gives farmers the ability to search for diseases and get the information about each disease .

Agents

WEBCHES also collects new information from Extension agents and publishes it only if the information is valid.

Sponsor

WEBCHES is a growing web application and would require donations to keep its growth for a better society.
